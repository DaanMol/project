"The Presidential Cheatsheet" offers an insight into the approval rating of the President of the United States. The overall timeline presents an overview into the history of the approval rating from 1940 to 2018. Selecting a president in this timeline will show the approval rating of the selected president. In this graph there are historical events, possibly giving an explanation for changes in the apparoval rating. Furthermore, a map of the presidential elections is shown, providing an image of the political division of the country.
